## Safety Summary

### Very Low Risk
- Posture correction
- Facial exercises
- Skincare routine
- Sleep optimization

### Low Risk
- Mewing (soft technique)
- Gua Sha (proper technique)
- Intermittent fasting (healthy individuals)

### Moderate Risk
- Chewing/gum exercises (TMJ stress)
- Hard mewing (excessive force)
- Extended fasting (without medical supervision)

### High Risk - EXCLUDED
- Bone smashing (not included in this library - too dangerous)
- Extreme force techniques
