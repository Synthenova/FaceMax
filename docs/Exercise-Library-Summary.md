## Exercise Format Standard

Every exercise document includes:

### Required Sections
1. **Overview** - Category, target areas, equipment
2. **How It Works** - Scientific/mechanical explanation
3. **Instructions**
   - Step-by-Step (5-6 steps)
   - Beginner Variation (duration, frequency, intensity)
   - Intermediate Variation (duration, frequency, intensity)
   - Advanced Variation (duration, frequency, intensity)
4. **Common Mistakes** - What to avoid
5. **Expected Results** - Timeline and outcomes
6. **Safety & Disclaimers**
   - Risk Level (Very Low/Low/Moderate/High)
   - Who Should Avoid
   - Warning Signs to Stop
   - Potential Side Effects
   - Medical Disclaimer
